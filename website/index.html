WovBot

World of Vanilla Bot was created as a modern tool to be used along side the WoW Classic experience. We hope that guilds will find the bot useful as a modern alternative to DKP or other loot-rolling currencies, as well as a provider of in-game information provided by the wow classic API.

WoVBot General Commands:

  • !setmain <Character> - Used to associate your WoW Main with your discord account. Must be done before any other WoVBot commands can be made.
  • !classicdb <Database> <Name> - Returns the searched item/spell's tooltip. Replace <Database> with Item to query items database, or Spell to query the spells database.

Lootcoin

LootCoin is WoVBots modern approach to an alternative to DKP. Since Vanilla, tecchies have happened upon blockchain technology. And LootCoin is a cryptocurrency designed exclusivley for WoW Classic which gives guild memebers bargaining chips in lue of items / services and of course, all important raid loot rolls.

LootCoin Commands:

  • !dkp - Returns your current DKP Balance. Must have !setmain first.
  • !transfer <@Mention> <Amount> - Transfers <amount> of dkp to the user mentioned.
